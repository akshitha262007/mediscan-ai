3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure
  • To run forensic analysis on uploaded documents and display results
  • To maintain your scan history within your account
  • To generate downloadable PDF forensic reports
  • To improve the platform's accuracy and reliability (anonymised, aggregated)

We do not use your documents to train AI models, sell data to third parties, or use data for advertising.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Uploaded files — stored on the server for analysis. You may delete them at any time from your dashboard (feature coming soon).
  • Forensic results — stored in our database and linked to your account.
  • Account data — retained as long as your account is active.
  • Inactive accounts — data will be purged after 12 months of inactivity.

5. Who We Share Data With

Nobody. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to outside parties. If we use third-party hosting (e.g., Railway, Render), those providers have their own privacy policies governing infrastructure-level data.

6. Your Rights (DPDPA 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Grievance — raise a complaint with us or with the Data Protection Board of India

To exercise any of these rights, email us at akshithajune@gmail.com. We will respond within 72 hours.

7. Cookies

MediScan AI uses a single session cookie (session) to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies are used. The session cookie expires when you sign out or close your browser (unless "Remember me" is selected).

8. Security

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords
  • All sessions are signed with a server-side secret key
  • File uploads are validated by extension and magic bytes before processing
  • Users can only access their own scan results — enforced at the route level

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. Do not upload documents containing sensitive patient PII unless you are authorised to do so.

9. Medical & Legal Disclaimer

MediScan AI is a forensic analysis tool for investigative support only.

Results generated by this platform — including forgery scores, ELA heatmaps, metadata findings, and OCR analysis — are not legal evidence and should not be used as the sole basis for any clinical, legal, insurance, or administrative determination.

All findings must be independently reviewed and validated by a qualified forensic expert or authorised professional before any action is taken. The developers of MediScan AI accept no liability for decisions made based on the platform's output.
